About

College is expensive. We've been there. Having to pay hundreds of dollars for books you'll only use once is both financially and emotionally crushing. Sure, you can sell them back to your university bookstore, but when they pay you pennies and resell them for hundreds of times that, clearly it isn't working out.

We created UBooks to get around all of that. We enable students to buy and sell books directly with each other without ridiculous price markups. Signing up with us allows you to search for books you need being sold by students just like you. Here at UBook, sell your own textbooks at a price you choose!
